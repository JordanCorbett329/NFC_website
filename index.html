Why Chiropractic?

A chiropractor specialises in diagnosing and treating neuromuscular disorders, with an emphasis on adjustments and/or manipulation of the spine. At NFC we focus on finding the cause of discomfort and supporting function — including care for newborns and children with simple home-care exercises.
